igitoki

banana

Soak beans for at least 3 hours in plenty of water. Drain, place in pan and boil for about 40 minutes until beans are tender. Peel and chop the green bananas. Add the beans and bananas to the oil. Add salt and pepper, stir and fry for 2 minutes. Add 1liter of water and simmer until the bananas are completely cooked and the water has reduced to about 250ml. Serve when still hot.
