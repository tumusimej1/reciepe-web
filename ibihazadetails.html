pumpkins

Remove the stem and fibrous material around the stem and the bottom of the pumpkin. Cube the pumpkin into large pieces. Place in a large pot with just enough water to steam the pumpkin. Boil for about 30 minutes or until both the pumpkin and the pumpkin skin are tender.
